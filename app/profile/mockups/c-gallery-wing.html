Detail. Detail. Detail. More detail.

I was sick of cards with no substance and/or wrong information. I wanted cards anyone could pick up as a baseline, modify to their liking, or use as-is.

Cardmaking Method

  • AI-assisted cardmaking with heavy human oversight.
  • Big-ish cards. (be happy you missed my allergic-to-lorebooks 8k-token phase)
  • Canonical detail. True to the character's lore, up to the mentioned time.
  • Embedded lorebooks. Keeps active tokens low.
  • Extra care where it counts most. Character voice, openings, and (my) scenario definitions.
