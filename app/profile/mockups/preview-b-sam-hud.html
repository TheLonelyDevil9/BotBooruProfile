I typically collect 20-50k+ tokens of mostly canonical (unless stated otherwise) character data, to consume and condense into every single card I release. Expect them to be foot-focused, large, fleshed-out, and hopefully as lore-accurate as possible (at least as of the time the card is released).

Even OCs are fleshed out similarly, to have a detailed personality and their own quirks, then condensed.

This is all done with a massive amount of heavy lifting done by AI.

Why do I do this? I was sick of cards with no substance and/or wrong information. I wanted cards anyone could pick up as a baseline, modify to their liking, or use as-is.
