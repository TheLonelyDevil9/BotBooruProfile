Detail. Detail. Detail. More detail.

Cardmaking Method:

  • AI-assisted, heavy human oversight.
  • Big-ish cards. (be happy you missed my allergic-to-lorebooks 8k-token phase)
  • Canonical detail. True to the character's lore, up to a confirmed moment in time.
  • Embedded lorebooks. Keeps active tokens low.
  • Care where it counts. Character voice, openings, scenario definitions.

Why? I was sick of cards with no substance or wrong information. I wanted cards anyone could pick up as a baseline, modify to their liking, or use as-is.
